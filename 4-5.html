4.5 Video Proses

Procedure Text

Tools & Materials:

  • Used paper
  • Scissors
  • Hot water (approximately 4 times the amount of used paper)
  • Large container (bucket or basin)
  • Blender
  • Cloth
  • Silkscreen
  • Dye (tea, flowers, etc.)

Steps to Make Recycled Paper:

  1. The necessary tools and materials should be gathered.
  2. The used paper should be torn into small pieces.
  3. The paper pieces should be soaked in hot water for 24 hours to soften their texture.
  4. After soaking, the paper is blended with water in a 1:3 ratio until a pulp-like texture is formed (not too watery).
  5. A bucket is prepared and filled with water (¼ or ½ full), then the pulp is mixed in.
  6. Natural dye can be added to give the paper color.
  7. The pulp is shaped using a silkscreen, adjusted to the desired paper size.
  8. The screen is flipped onto a flat surface and the pulp gently pressed for evenness.
  9. The paper is transferred to a board, covered with cloth, and pressed with weight to smoothen the texture.
  10. The paper is dried under the sun.
  11. Once completely dry, the cloth is removed and the recycled paper is ready for use.
